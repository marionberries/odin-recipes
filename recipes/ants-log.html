Ants on a Log

Better than actual ants on an actual log!

Ants on a log make for a great snack due to the protein packed peanut butter, sweet raisins, and the low calorie crunch of the celery.

Ingredients:

  • Celery
  • Peanut butter
  • Raisins

Instructions

  1. Grab your celery
  2. Spread your peanut butter in the groove of the celery
  3. Stick your raisins on the peanut butter
